Make at home

Save money. Control quality. Cleaner than store-bought. All from regular toned/full-fat milk and basic pantry items.

  • Curd / Dahi — 1 cup
    Boil 200ml full-fat milk, cool to body temp (warm but not hot to touch). Stir in 1 tsp starter curd, cover with lid. Leave at room temp 6–8 hrs (longer in winter). Refrigerate once set.
  • Greek yogurt / Hung curd — 120g
    Tie 200g regular curd in clean muslin/cotton cloth, hang over a bowl 2–3 hrs at room temp. The whey drips out, leaving thick creamy yogurt. Use straight in smoothie bowls and lassis.
  • Buttermilk / Chaas — 200ml
    Whisk 50g curd with 150ml chilled water till frothy. Add ½ tsp roasted jeera powder, pinch of black salt, 4–5 chopped curry leaves, coriander. Serve cold.
  • Paneer — 100g
    Boil 500ml full-fat milk. Once boiling, lower flame, add 1 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar, stir gently. Milk will split into curds + whey within 30 sec. Strain through muslin, rinse with cold water to remove sourness, gather edges and press flat under a heavy bowl for 30 min.
  • Sprouted moong — 100g
    Soak 50g green moong overnight in plenty of water. Drain, rinse, tie in slightly damp cloth. Leave in warm spot (kitchen counter) for 24–36 hrs. Done when 0.5–1 cm white sprouts appear. Refrigerate up to 3 days.
  • Sattu drink — 250ml
    Whisk 1.5 tbsp roasted sattu powder into 250ml chilled water till smooth and lumpless. Add juice of ½ lemon, pinch of black salt, ½ tsp roasted jeera powder, chopped mint. Serve immediately over ice.

Craving killers

For when sugar urges hit. Most of these stop the craving in 5 minutes.

  • Chew 1 tsp jeera + saunf — kills sweet cravings fast
  • Cinnamon + monk fruit in warm water
  • Hung curd + monk fruit + cardamom = "shrikhand"
  • Frozen blueberries straight from freezer
  • Black coffee with cinnamon + monk fruit
  • Brush teeth — the mint nukes cravings instantly
  • Max 2 dry dates only if truly desperate
  • Sleep 7+ hours — under-sleep doubles next-day cravings
🌱

Anti-bloat protocol

Build these habits in slowly. They compound over weeks.

  • Use cumin, ajwain, hing, fennel daily
  • Avoid raw onion, raw garlic, raw cabbage
  • No ice-cold water with meals
  • Chew slowly — 20 chews per bite
  • Walk 5–10 min after every main meal
  • Stop eating 2.5 hrs before bed
  • Cook cruciferous well — don't eat raw
  • One glass buttermilk daily for gut health

Stone-safe swaps

Low-oxalate alternatives to common high-oxalate foods.

  • Spinach → mushroom, broccoli, cauliflower
  • Beetroot → carrot, capsicum
  • Peanuts → 5 soaked almonds + 2 walnuts
  • Sweet potato → carrot, pumpkin (limited)
  • Black tea → green tea, herbal infusion
  • Raw cocoa → cinnamon, cardamom
  • Okra → green beans, cabbage
  • 3–3.5L water daily flushes oxalates

Rules for drinking nights

  • 1 glass water between each drink
